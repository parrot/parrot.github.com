TITLE

regex.pir - The <regex> subrule

DESCRIPTION

regex(PMC mob)

Handles parsing of "slash regexes" -- i.e., regexes that are terminated by a slash. For this, we just call PGE's p6 regex parser, telling it to stop parsing on the closing slash.
