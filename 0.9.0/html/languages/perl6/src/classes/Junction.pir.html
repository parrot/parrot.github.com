!DISPATCH_JUNCTION
Does a junctional dispatch. XXX Needs to support named args.
!DISPATCH_JUNCTION_SINGLE
Wrapper for junction dispatcher in the single dispatch case, where we are passed the sub that is being called along with a way to build tuples of the parameters for the dispatcher.
!DISPATCH_JUNCTION_MULTI
Wrapper for junction dispatcher in the multi dispatch case. Here we are handed back as the thingy to call in place of a candidate, and PCC doesn't give us an easy way to unshift another argument into the call, so we have it attached as a property.
